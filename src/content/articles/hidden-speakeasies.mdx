Shh! Don't tell anyone, but Time Out Israel has rounded up the five best speakeasy bars in Tel Aviv. Step out of your cozy comfortable zone and into the world of American pop-culture at these hidden bars where the art of mixology is no joke.

## 1. Butler

The quaint one-room bar nestled inside Dallal is a visual overload. The walls are adorned with period pieces like paintings, key holders, and other gems. While Bellboy asks for cocktails and enjoys serving mimosas, Butler knows how to spotlight the cocktail itself — in many forms. Bartenders serve the "Coffee Cocktail," a rich Italian-inspired creation, and the "Harakiri," dressed with subtle notes of exotic spice.

## 2. Jasper John's

With cocktail bars popping up left and right on Dizengoff, it can be easy to miss this slightly hidden spot. Jasper John's is a cozy little bar with armchairs and creative cocktails with names like "Plan Aperol" and "Black Magic." They've adopted the unofficial Italian happy hour "Aperitivo" — order between 19:00 and 20:00 and get free tapas.
