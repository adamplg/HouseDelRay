Love is in the air at House of Delray. This Valentine's Day, we're pulling out all the stops with an exclusive evening designed for couples who appreciate the finer things.

## The Evening

Arrive to a welcome cocktail on the rooftop terrace as the sun sets over Tel Aviv. Then descend to the restaurant for a five-course tasting menu crafted by our chef, featuring the freshest ingredients from the Carmel Market paired with boutique Israeli wines.

## Live Music

A jazz duo will serenade diners throughout the evening with a mix of classics and original compositions. The perfect soundtrack for a night to remember.
