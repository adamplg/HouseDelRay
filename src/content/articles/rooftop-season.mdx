There's something about a Tel Aviv rooftop that makes everything better. The golden light, the Mediterranean breeze, the sound of the city humming below. And now, the Delray rooftop is officially open for the season.

## What's New This Year

We've expanded the bar menu with a new collection of seasonal cocktails crafted by our head mixologist. Think watermelon and basil spritz, smoked pineapple margarita, and a refreshing cucumber gin fizz.

## Events Calendar

Every Thursday evening from May through October, we're hosting sunset DJ sets with a rotating lineup of local and international artists. Check our events page for the full schedule.
